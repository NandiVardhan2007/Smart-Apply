Setup Instructions

1
Download & unzip the extension
Click "Download Extension" above and unzip to a permanent folder.
2
Load in Chrome
Go to chrome://extensions → Enable Developer Mode → Load Unpacked → select the folder.
3
Sign in
Use your SmartApply email & password to sign in directly in the extension popup.
4
Start applying on LinkedIn
Visit LinkedIn Jobs, open the extension popup, and click Start Bot.
