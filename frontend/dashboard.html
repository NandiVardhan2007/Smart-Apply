Resumes & Routing Keywords

Upload New
Add routing keywords to each resume. When the bot sees a job title matching your keywords, it automatically picks that resume. As a software student — upload separate resumes for Python, Java, Frontend, etc. and tag them with relevant keywords.

How Routing Works

Bot reads the job title (e.g. "Python Developer at TCS") and checks each resume's keywords.
The first resume whose keyword matches the job title is used. If none match, the most recently uploaded is the fallback.
Keywords are partial and case-insensitive — "react" matches "React Developer", "ReactJS", "React Native".
