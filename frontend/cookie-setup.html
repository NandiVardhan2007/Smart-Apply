LinkedIn Session Setup

Log in to LinkedIn on your personal laptop, export your session cookies using the script below, paste them here, and the bot will use your authenticated session — no password needed on the server.

Why this works: LinkedIn blocks headless browsers trying to log in from new IPs. By exporting your existing session cookies from a browser where you're already logged in, the bot inherits that trusted session and skips the login step entirely. Cookies typically last 1–2 years before needing a refresh.
1

Open LinkedIn in your browser

Go to linkedin.com and make sure you're logged in to your account.

2

Open Developer Tools → Console

Press F12 (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Option+J (Mac) to open DevTools, then click the Console tab.

3

Paste and run this script

Copy the code below, paste it into the console, and press Enter. It will copy your LinkedIn cookies to your clipboard automatically.

copy(JSON.stringify(document.cookie.split(';').map(c=>{const[n,...v]=c.trim().split('=');return{name:n,value:v.join('='),domain:'.linkedin.com',path:'/',secure:true,httpOnly:false,sameSite:'None'}}), null, 2))
4

Paste the result below and save

Your clipboard now contains the cookie JSON. Paste it in the box below and click Save.
